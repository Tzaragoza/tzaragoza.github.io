About

I am a doctoral researcher and ATER in Computer Science at the University of Pau and Pays de l’Adour (UPPA), affiliated with the LIUPPA laboratory in France.

My research lies at the intersection of software engineering and sustainable computing, with a focus on Green IT and user-centered approaches to energy efficiency. I study how software systems influence human behavior and how adaptive feedback, data-driven models, and AI techniques can be leveraged to reduce the energy footprint of software usage.

My interests include Software Engineering, Green IT & Sustainability, Behavioral Computer Science, Autonomic Computing, and Artificial Intelligence, with an emphasis on empirical evaluation and real-world deployment.
